Taco Bell-Style Mexican Pizza

You certainly can't say Taco Bell isn't versatile. A handful of ingredients, smashed into each other and Frankensteined into more-or-less novel items. This one claims to be a pizza. Mamma mia, or whatever.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 cup refried beans, warmed
  • 1 cup cooked, taco-seasoned ground beef
  • 1/2 cup taco sauce
  • 1/4 cup red enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp sliced black olives, if you hate yourself
  • 3 tbsp chopped green onions

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil over high heat. Lightly fry each tortilla to golden brown, turning frequently and adding oil as necessary
  2. Preheat oven to 400F (200C)
  3. Place 4 tortillas on a baking sheet and spread 3 tbsp of both refried beans and ground beef evenly over each. Place the remaining tortillas on top.
  4. Mix the taco sauce and enchilada sauce in a small bowl. Spread 2 tbsp onto each of the tortillas and sprinkle 1/4 cup of each cheese on top.
  5. Bake until the cheese has melted, about 3 to 4 minutes. Remove and top with diced tomatoes and green onions. Throw those olives in the trash where they belong.
